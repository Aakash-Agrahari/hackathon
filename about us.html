About Us

Eventifier, a company that has been established with the aim to

serve as the global platform to connect both event management

companies as well as event attendees through a single platform.

Our vision is to merge every event companies at a same place so

that everyone could get access to every event(music, dance,

comedy skit and many more...) that will be conducted at their

local place, city, state, country or globally.
